Recruitment

Cornell Hyperloop is a for-credit Engineering Project Team and our mission is to contribute to the development of Hyperloop technology. Every semester, we look to recruit new talented and ambitious students to help us build the future of transportation.


Applications are currently OPEN for freshmen until October 1st. The deadline for sophomores, juniors, and seniors has already passed.

Who We Look For

Cornell Hyperloop has a position for everyone! We are broken down into mechanical engineering, electrical/software engineering, and business/web development teams, which are then further divided into a total of seven "sub-teams," each focusing on a specific component of our work. Each team recruits undergraduate students of all years from any major that can demonstrate the skills needed to succeed on that team. To learn more about what each sub-team focuses on, visit our team page.
