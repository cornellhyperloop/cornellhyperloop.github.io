Braking


The Braking team designs the magnetic and mechanical braking systems of the pod and integrates them into the pod. The team handles the careful manipulation of the Halbach arrays which make up the magnetic braking system by inducing eddy currents within the conductive rail.

The Propulsion team designs the components of the pod's propulsion system, featuring an all-electric motor drivetrain. The aerodynamic reinforced carbon fiber shell enable the pod to achieve high travel velocities while successfully minimizing drag and guaranteeing structural integrity.
